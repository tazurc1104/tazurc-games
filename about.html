About Us:

Owner of tazurC Games

TazurC Games is a website created by Gabriel Angelo D. Cruzat, a 23-year-old student from the Philippines who loves playing games and web technologies. Gabriel is currently a 4th-year graduating student and an aspiring front-end web developer. Creating websites is not just his hobby, it's also his passion. With TazurC Games, Gabriel has brought his love for games and web technologies together to create a platform that showcases his skills and creativity. While the games on TazurC Games are created using basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, they are anything but basic when it comes to entertainment value. Gabriel has designed each game with careful attention to detail, ensuring that they are engaging and fun to play. Whether you're a hardcore gamer or just looking for a casual way to pass the time, TazurC Games has something for everyone.
